About AutoCore

Welcome to AutoCore! We are a team of passionate car enthusiasts dedicated to delivering high-quality, in-depth reviews of modern vehicles. Our mission is to help readers make informed decisions through honest analysis, technical insight, and a love for automotive design. Whether it's electric innovation or high-performance engineering, we bring clarity to complexity.

Our Story

AutoCore was founded in 2025 by longtime friends and automotive aficionados Andras Marko and Viktor Bercik. What started as casual car conversations soon evolved into a full-scale review platform. Frustrated by shallow, ad-driven car content, they set out to build a source of trustworthy reviews, written for true car lovers. Through countless test drives and long nights editing content, AutoCore was born—and continues to grow.

Our Achievements

In just our first year, AutoCore has reached over 3,000 monthly readers from around the world. We’ve partnered with industry professionals, collaborated with photographers, and even been featured in niche automotive publications. Our Instagram audience exceeds 2.5k followers and continues to grow. We're proud of the trust our readers place in us—and we’re just getting started.
