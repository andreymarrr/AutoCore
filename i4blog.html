BMW i4 review

02.04.2025

Exterior

The 2025 BMW i4 continues to build on the brand’s progressive electric design language. The front showcases a bold, enclosed kidney grille with integrated sensors, giving the car a futuristic but unmistakably BMW look. Slim LED headlights and sharp daytime running lights enhance road presence and visibility. The sleek silhouette and gently sloped roofline deliver coupe-like elegance, while aerodynamic refinements, including active air flaps and flush door handles, improve efficiency. New alloy wheel options and subtle blue accents hint at the car’s electric nature. The rear features sculpted LED taillights and a diffuser-style bumper, completing the i4’s sporty yet refined aesthetic.

Interior

Step inside the 2025 BMW i4 and you’re greeted by a tech-forward, minimalist cabin. The curved dual-display integrates a 12.3-inch instrument panel and a 14.9-inch infotainment screen running BMW’s iDrive 8.5 system. The dashboard and door trims feature sustainable materials, while the ambient lighting adds a customizable and premium feel. The seats offer excellent support and adjustability, even on long drives. Rear passengers benefit from generous legroom, considering the car’s sporty profile. Sound insulation has been further improved for a quiet EV ride, and the panoramic sunroof enhances cabin spaciousness. Overall, the interior blends innovation with typical BMW comfort and quality.

Engine

The i4 comes in multiple variants, including the i4 eDrive40 and the performance-oriented i4 M50. The eDrive40 produces 335 horsepower and offers up to 300 miles of range on a single charge. The dual-motor M50 variant delivers 536 horsepower and accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.7 seconds. Both models offer responsive handling and precise steering, staying true to BMW’s sporty roots.

Summary

The 2025 BMW i4 is a compelling entry in the premium electric sedan segment. It merges performance, luxury, and cutting-edge tech with the brand’s unmistakable driving character. Whether you’re looking for long-range efficiency or exhilarating acceleration, the i4 delivers. Its refined interior and modern design elevate the overall experience, making it a standout daily driver. For those seeking an electric vehicle that doesn’t compromise on driving dynamics, the i4 is an excellent choice.
