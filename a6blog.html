Audi A6 review

14.02.2025

Exterior

The 2025 Audi A6 C9 debuts with a refreshed, sharper design that blends sophistication and innovation. Its front grille is wider and flatter, flanked by new digital LED headlights with customizable light signatures. The body features clean lines, subtle creases, and refined chrome detailing for a premium, understated appearance. Aerodynamics have been subtly improved with sculpted side skirts and a sleek roofline that tapers gently toward the rear. At the back, dynamic OLED taillights and a more integrated diffuser give the A6 a confident, modern presence. The overall look is refined but unmistakably Audi—balanced between executive elegance and sporty intent.

Interior

Inside, the A6 C9 offers a sanctuary of comfort and advanced technology. The cabin features Audi’s signature horizontal layout with dual touchscreens and a fully digital Virtual Cockpit. Materials include premium leather, real wood veneers, brushed aluminum, and optional ambient lighting for a tailored atmosphere. The MMI system is faster and more intuitive, offering haptic feedback and natural voice commands. Passengers in both rows enjoy generous space, improved seat ergonomics, and whisper-quiet ride quality thanks to enhanced insulation. With wireless charging, advanced climate zones, and built-in Alexa integration, the A6’s interior is as intelligent as it is elegant.

Engine

The 2025 A6 offers a range of mild-hybrid and plug-in hybrid options. The standard 2.0 TFSI engine produces 261 horsepower and pairs with a 7-speed S tronic transmission and quattro all-wheel drive. Higher trims offer a 3.0-liter V6 with 340 horsepower and enhanced torque distribution. Efficiency has been improved across the board, and plug-in hybrid variants now provide a longer all-electric range. The ride remains smooth yet responsive, with adaptive suspension available for added refinement.

Summary

The Audi A6 C9 2025 represents the perfect blend of executive comfort and progressive technology. With a sharp new design, smart hybrid powertrains, and a cabin that feels like a digital lounge, it’s built for drivers who demand luxury without shouting. Whether you’re commuting or cruising on the autobahn, the A6 delivers calm confidence, seamless tech, and Audi’s trademark sophistication. It’s not just a business sedan—it’s a smart statement on four wheels.
