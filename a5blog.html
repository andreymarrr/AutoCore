Audi A5 review

06.04.2025

Exterior

The 2025 Audi A5 introduces a bold evolution in exterior styling. The front fascia features a wider, more aggressive grille with a honeycomb pattern. Sleek matrix LED headlights enhance visibility and add a futuristic edge. Subtle contour lines along the side emphasize the car’s aerodynamic profile. The redesigned rear showcases slimmer taillights and a more sculpted bumper. Audi offers new alloy wheel designs that perfectly complement the body lines. Overall, the exterior strikes a balance between elegance and sportiness. It’s a clear statement of modern German engineering.

Interior

Inside the new A5, Audi continues its tradition of refinement and cutting-edge technology. The cockpit is driver-oriented, with a new 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and a 10.1-inch infotainment touchscreen. Materials throughout the cabin have been upgraded, with soft-touch surfaces, aluminum trim, and optional wood inlays. The seats are more supportive and comfortable, especially during longer trips. Ambient lighting allows for a customizable atmosphere at night. Rear-seat passengers benefit from increased legroom and USB-C charging ports. Audi has also improved cabin insulation for a quieter ride. The interior is a luxurious, high-tech environment.

Engine

The 2025 Audi A5 comes standard with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine. It produces 201 horsepower and delivers power smoothly through a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission. For those seeking more performance, the 261-hp version with quattro all-wheel drive is available. Both configurations offer excellent fuel efficiency and responsive handling.

Summary

The 2025 Audi A5 is a well-rounded luxury coupe with sharp looks and strong performance. Its refreshed design makes it stand out on the road while maintaining Audi’s classic elegance. The interior offers advanced tech and high-end comfort. Engine options provide flexibility for both casual and performance-oriented drivers. Overall, it’s a compelling choice for anyone seeking a premium driving experience in a sleek package.
